Biography

I am a PhD student at the Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), affiliated with the HUST Media Lab (Intelligent Media Computing and Cybersecurity Laboratory) (智能媒体计算与网络安全实验室). I am advised by Junqing Yu and Zikai Song.

My research interests lie in AI for Social Science (AI4SS), especially social simulation and neuro-symbolic reasoning.

Prior to this, I received my bachelor's degree from the School of Cyber Science and Engineering, Huazhong University of Science and Technology, in June 2024, where I was advised by Bin Yuan.
