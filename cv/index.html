Biography

I am a PhD student at the Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), affiliated with the Intelligent Media Computing and Cybersecurity Laboratory (智能媒体计算与网络安全实验室). My supervisors are Junqing Yu and Zikai Song.

My research focuses on computational social science, neuro-symbolic reasoning, and social network analysis.
