About

I am a PhD student at the Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), working in the HUST Media Lab (Intelligent Media Computing and Cybersecurity Laboratory) (智能媒体计算与网络安全实验室). My research is supervised by Junqing Yu and Zikai Song.

My research focuses on AI for Social Science (AI4SS), with particular interests in social simulation and neuro-symbolic reasoning.

I received my bachelor's degree from the School of Cyber Science and Engineering, Huazhong University of Science and Technology, in June 2024, under the supervision of Bin Yuan.
